# Slideshow Mode - Updated Guide

## What Changed

The slideshow mode has been updated for a cleaner, more immersive experience:

### ✅ What's New
- **Hidden Controls**: Controls are now hidden by default
- **Hover to Show**: Move your mouse to reveal controls
- **Smooth Transitions**: Controls fade in/out smoothly
- **Cleaner Interface**: Minimal UI for maximum focus

### ❌ What's Removed
- **Pause/Resume**: No longer available
- **Speed Control**: Fixed at 3 seconds per page
- **Timer Display**: Countdown timer removed

## How to Use

### Start Slideshow
1. Click **"▶ Slideshow"** button in header
2. Full-screen slideshow activates
3. Pages auto-advance every 3 seconds
4. Controls are hidden

### Show Controls
- **Move your mouse** over the page
- Controls appear at bottom
- Page info appears at top-right
- Exit hint appears at bottom-left

### Navigate
- Click **"← Prev"** to go back
- Click **"Next →"** to go forward
- Click **"✕ Exit"** to exit

### Exit Slideshow
- Click **"✕ Exit"** button, OR
- Press **ESC** key, OR
- Click anywhere on page

## Controls Layout

```
                    Page 1 / 10
                    (top-right, on hover)

        [Large PDF Page Display]


← Prev    Next →    ✕ Exit
(bottom center, on hover)

Press ESC or click to exit
(bottom-left, on hover)
```
